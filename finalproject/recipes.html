Recipe Snapshots

Nigeria vs Ghana vs Senegal

Nigeria 🇳🇬

Smoky Party Jollof

Parboiled Basmati Rice Tomato-Pepper Base Thyme & Curry Chicken
  • Blend tomatoes, peppers, onion, and scotch bonnet.
  • Cook with tomato paste and seasonings.
  • Add stock and rice, then steam until fluffy and smoky.
  • Serve with chicken and fried plantain.
Ghana 🇬🇭

Well-Spiced Stew Base

Long-Grain Rice Tomato Stew Bay Leaves Carrots Bell Pepper
  • Create a rich tomato stew base.
  • Add garlic, ginger, onions, and spices.
  • Cook rice slowly in seasoned stock.
  • Serve with grilled chicken or vegetables.
Senegal 🇸🇳

Thieboudienne (Ceebu Jën)

Broken Rice Fish & Vegetables Nététou
  • Prepare a tomato-onion fish stew.
  • Add vegetables and aromatic seasonings.
  • Steam broken rice in the flavorful broth.
  • Serve with fish and vegetables.

What Makes Each Recipe Unique?

Nigeria: Known for its smoky "party jollof" flavor and rich tomato-pepper sauce.
Ghana: Popular for its fragrant spices and carefully layered flavors.
Senegal: Considered by many to be the historical origin of Jollof rice.
